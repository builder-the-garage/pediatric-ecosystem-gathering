Three Lenses
What We're Really Here to Work On

These aren't fixed tracks — they're starting frames. The goal is to reframe at least one before we're done.

Chapter 01
The Translation Gap

Brilliant science is dying between bench and bedside. Who owns the handoff — and what would it take to fund the crossing differently?

"We can generate the evidence. We just can't afford to generate it fast enough."

Chapter 02
Capital & Commercialization

Money exists but is misaligned. Investors, payers, and health systems each have a different definition of "ready." What does closing that gap look like?

"We're not struggling to find great science. We're struggling to get it funded at the right moment."

Chapter 03
Ecosystem Architecture

Platforms and programs keep getting built. Founders keep not using them. What does a hub that actually works look like — and who has to run it?

"We can't keep building digital bulletin boards and calling it infrastructure."
